हमारी कहानी · Our Story

Crafted with
Devotion

AGNI was born from Chef Priya Sharma's lifelong obsession with the sacred role of fire in Indian cooking. She spent fifteen years traversing the spice markets of Old Delhi, the coconut groves of Kerala, the royal kitchens of Rajasthan, and the street corners of Kolkata.

Today, our tandoor burns 24 hours a day — never extinguished. Every naan is hand-slapped and kissed by that ancient fire. Every curry begins with a tempering of whole spices in clarified butter. This is India as your grandmother would recognize it.

🔥 24-Hour Tandoor Our clay oven never goes cold — fired by traditional charcoal
🌿 Fresh Ground Masalas All spice blends ground in-house every morning
🏺 Heirloom Recipes Dishes traced back 3–5 generations across India's regions
🏆 Award-Winning Times Food Award, India's Best Restaurant 2022 & 2024

प्रिया शर्मा

The Maestri

Spice is Her Mother Tongue

Chef Priya Sharma grew up in her grandmother's kitchen in Agra, learning that cooking is an act of love. After training at IHM Mumbai and staging at iconic restaurants across London and Singapore, she returned to India with a singular mission — to honour the breadth of Indian cuisine without diluting its soul.

Winner of the Times Food Award for Best Chef in India (2022 & 2024), she was featured on Netflix's Street Food India and holds a permanent residency in Condé Nast Traveller's top 20 Indian Restaurants.
